Flowers photos

Flowers are an essential part of flowering plants. They are responsible for reproduction and the development of fruits. Flowers are also widely appreciated for their beauty and fragrance, and are used in decorations, religious offerings, and celebrations.

Roses pictures

Roses belong to the genus Rosa in the family Rosaceae. They are known for their layered petals, rich fragrance, and various colors. Scientifically, they consist of sepals, petals, stamens, and carpels, and are widely cultivated for ornamental and commercial purposes.
